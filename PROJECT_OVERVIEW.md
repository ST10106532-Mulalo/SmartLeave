# SmartLeave - Enterprise Leave Management System
## Project Overview & Problem Statement

---

## 📌 The Problem

### Current Challenges in Leave Management

Many organizations still rely on manual processes for managing employee leave, resulting in:

**1. Inefficiency & Time Waste**
- Employees submit paper forms or send emails
- Managers spend hours tracking approvals
- HR manually updates spreadsheets
- Average time to process a leave request: 2-5 days

**2. Lack of Transparency**
- Employees don't know request status
- Managers have no centralized view of team availability
- No visibility into team attendance patterns

**3. Poor Communication**
- Late arrivals go unreported
- No systematic way to notify managers
- Missed overtime opportunities

**4. No Real-Time Insights**
- Cannot identify leave trends
- No data for workforce planning
- Difficulty tracking absenteeism patterns

**5. Security & Compliance Risks**
- Sensitive employee data in emails/spreadsheets
- No audit trail for approvals
- No role-based access control

---

## 💡 The Solution

### SmartLeave: A Modern, Integrated Leave Management Platform

SmartLeave addresses these challenges with:

### 1. Automated Workflow
- Employees submit requests online in seconds
- Managers receive instant notifications
- Approvals processed in minutes, not days
- Status updates visible in real-time

### 2. Role-Based Access
- **Employees:** Self-service leave applications, late reporting
- **Managers:** Approval dashboard, team overview
- **Supervisors:** Team pending requests, forwarding capabilities
- **Admins:** Full system control, user management

### 3. Real-Time Communication
- Instant notifications for new requests
- Sound alerts for important updates
- Visual indicators (red flash for overtime)
- Email fallback notifications

### 4. Complete Transparency
- Track every request from submission to approval
- View entire history of leaves and late arrivals
- Department-level analytics and reports

### 5. Overtime Management
- Managers post opportunities
- Department-specific visibility
- Employees apply with reasons
- Manager approval workflow
- Red flash badge for new opportunities

### 6. Late Arrival Reporting
- Employees report with reason and photo evidence
- Real-time manager notification
- Review and track status
